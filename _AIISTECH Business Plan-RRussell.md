  **Business Plan for AIISTECH Org — AI‑Driven PSA Platform**

## **1\. Executive Summary**

AIISTECH Org is an AI‑powered Professional Services Automation (PSA) platform designed to transform how service‑based organizations operate. The platform unifies project management, resource planning, time tracking, billing, and analytics into a single intelligent system. By combining a no‑code front‑end with custom microservices, AIISTECH Org delivers rapid iteration, enterprise‑grade scalability, and deep workflow automation.

The mission is simple: **eliminate operational friction** so teams can focus on delivering value, not managing administrative overhead. AIISTECH Org uses automation and predictive intelligence to streamline workflows, reduce revenue leakage, and provide real‑time insights that drive profitability.

---

## **2\. Problem Statement**

Professional service organizations face increasing pressure to deliver faster, more accurately, and with greater transparency. Yet most firms struggle with:

* Disconnected tools for project management, time tracking, invoicing, and reporting  
* Manual processes that slow down delivery and introduce errors  
* Limited visibility into resource utilization and profitability  
* High administrative overhead  
* Lack of predictive insights for planning and forecasting  
* Difficulty scaling operations without adding more staff

These challenges lead to missed deadlines, inconsistent client experiences, and revenue loss.

---

## **3\. Solution Overview**

AIISTECH  Org provides a unified PSA platform with **AI‑driven automation** embedded throughout the entire lifecycle of service delivery.

### **Core Capabilities**

* **Automated Client Onboarding**  
   Smart forms, document collection, identity verification, and workflow triggers.  
* **Project & Task Management**  
   Create, assign, track, and close projects with automated updates and rule‑based workflows.  
* **AI‑Enhanced Time Tracking**  
   Predictive time suggestions, auto‑logging, and anomaly detection.  
* **Billing & Invoicing Automation**  
   Auto‑generated invoices, usage‑based billing, payment reminders, and reconciliation.  
* **Resource Allocation Optimization**  
   AI forecasts workload, availability, and skill matching.  
* **Analytics & Reporting**  
   Real‑time dashboards, profitability insights, forecasting, and anomaly alerts.

### **AI Features**

* Natural language task creation  
* Predictive analytics for resource planning  
* Automated documentation generation  
* Intelligent routing and approvals  
* Anomaly detection in budgets, time logs, and performance metrics  
* Workflow automation engine with event‑driven triggers
